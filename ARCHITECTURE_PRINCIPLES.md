## 1. AI Creates Two Kinds Of Value

AI creates two kinds of value: Decision Support and Learning Assets.

Decision Support helps recruiters complete the current recruiting decision.

Learning Assets turn verified recruiting experience into long-term organizational assets. These assets continuously improve Job Profiles, Talent Maps, recruiting strategy, and AI capabilities.

中文说明：

- Decision Support：帮助招聘人员完成当前的招聘决策。
- Learning Assets：把经过验证的经验沉淀为企业长期资产，用于持续优化岗位画像、人才地图、招聘策略和 AI 能力。

Rules:

- Decision Support must not become organizational memory automatically.
- Learning Assets must be reviewed, versioned, explainable, and auditable.
- AI may assist, analyze, summarize, compare, and recommend.
- AI must not make final hiring decisions or bypass recruiter review.
